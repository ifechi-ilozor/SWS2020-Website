About SWS & The Brown Chapter

Educating
Since 2006

Smart Woman Securities (SWS) is a not-for-profit organization focused on finance education for undergraduate women. Through seminars, workshops, mentorship and exposure to successful professionals and businesses, SWS provides a foundation upon which women can build greater knowledge of the finance industry.

Here at Brown, our goal is to guide as many women and femme-identifying students as we can to financial literacy. After members have that foundation, we enable them to explore many different paths in finance, which can be more focused on professional careers and opportunities or on personal investing and development.
