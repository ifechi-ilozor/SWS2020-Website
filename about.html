About Us

Learn more about our mission, timeline & goals, and leadership.

Our Mission:

To lead as many women and femme-identifying students as we possibly can to financial literacy.
To demistify finance and empower students to write their own stories in this industry.
To provide paths and opportunities for students to explore and find their own space in finance.

How Far We've Come & Where We Are Going

The Brown University chapter of Smart Woman Securities was founded in 2011. The chapter started off by offering the Fall Lecture Series, which is a program that every single chapter across the nation also holds.

As the club grew, SWS began to offer more general body events such as our kickoffs and showcases (lots of food of course!) as well as more workshops and collaborations. The Research Committee was also launched.

SWS added more value to the finance community on campus with internship panels, resume and cover letter reviews, free LinkedIn headshots, more external competition and conference opportunities.

To fulfill our mission, we are looking to partner with clubs on and off campus as well as companies who believe in diversifying the finance industry and closing the financial literacy gap to provide more value.

Our most immediate goal is continuing to support our members and the Brown community during the COVID-19 panidemic. We hope to continue enriching members through our curriculum and events, even if that means restructuring things to fit a new and virtual format.
